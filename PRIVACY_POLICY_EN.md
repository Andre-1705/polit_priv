# Privacy Policy - Social Metrics Automation

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Social Metrics Automation ("we", "our", or "the application"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our social metrics automation application.

By using Social Metrics Automation, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Data:** Name, email address, username, password (encrypted).
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture, bio, user preferences.
- **Social Media Credentials:** Access tokens and permissions you authorize to connect your social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, etc.).
- **User-Generated Content:** Posts, comments, automation settings, created templates.
- **Payment Information:** Credit/debit card data, billing information (processed through secure payment providers).

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, interactions with the application.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser, IP address, unique device identifiers.
- **Location Data:** Approximate geographic location based on your IP address (if enabled).
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** Cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and local storage.
- **Server Logs:** Error logs, performance metrics, diagnostic data.

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

- **Social Media Data:** Metrics, statistics, follower information, engagement, audience demographics obtained from social media APIs you connect.
- **Authentication Providers:** Public profile information when you use third-party login services (Google, Facebook, etc.).

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 8.1 General Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, transferable format
- **Objection:** Object to certain types of processing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw your consent at any time

### 8.2 Region-Specific Rights

**GDPR (EU/EEA):**
- Right to lodge complaints with your local data protection authority
- Right not to be subject to automated decisions

**CCPA (California):**
- Right to know what personal information we collect
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of selling information (not applicable, we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**LGPD (Brazil):**
- Right to confirm the existence of data processing
- Right to anonymization, blocking, or deletion
- Right to review automated decisions

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@socialmetricsautomation.com

## 9. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

### 9.1 Technical Measures

- **Encryption:** SSL/TLS for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) available
- **Tokenization:** Encrypted access tokens for social media credentials
- **Firewalls and Network Security:** Perimeter protection and continuous monitoring
- **Regular Backups:** Encrypted and redundant backups

### 9.2 Organizational Measures

- Restricted access based on principle of least privilege
- Regular security and privacy training for employees
- Confidentiality agreements with all employees
- Periodic security audits and penetration testing
- Security incident response plan

### 9.3 Limitations

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
